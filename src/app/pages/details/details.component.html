Types of Coconut Tree

  • West Coast Tall Coconut: Reaches up to 100 feet
  • Chandrakalpa Coconut: Grows between 50-60 feet
  • East Coast Tall Coconut: Typically 90 feet tall
  • Panama Tall Coconut: Also around 90 feet in height
  • Macapuno Coconut: Shorter variety, about 16 feet tall

Comprehensive Guide to Identifying Coconut Trees

  • Leaves: Long, pinnate leaves with feather-like leaflets
  • Flowers: Large clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers in creamy yellow or light green
  • Fruits: Large, round coconuts with fibrous husk and hard shell containing edible meat and water
  • Trunk & Bark: Smooth, grayish-brown cylindrical trunk thickening at the base
  • Height: 60-100 feet
